Here is a collection of random, interesting, and lesser-known facts about the sport of cricket, spanning its history, rules, and records.

Cricket is a globally popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players on a large field, with a central 22-yard pitch. Originating in southeast England, it is now a major international sport governed by the ICC

The primary objective is for the batting team to score more runs than the opposing team, which bowls and fields to restrict the score and take wickets.

Matches are played in three main formats: Test, ODI (One Day International), and T20. It is not just a game, but a sport that promotes teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.
